Best Practices for Studying

  • Create a study schedule and stick to it.
  • Take regular breaks to stay refreshed.
  • Focus on understanding concepts, not just memorizing.
  • Stay hydrated and eat healthy for better concentration.
  • Group study sessions can help reinforce learning.

Stay Motivated!
